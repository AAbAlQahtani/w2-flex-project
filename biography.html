Biography

The life journey of Stephen Fry from childhood to global fame

The Life of Stephen Fry

Basic Information

Full Name: Stephen John Fry

Date of Birth: 24 August 1957

Place of Birth: Hampstead, London, England

Nationality: British

Occupation: Actor, writer, comedian, presenter

Notable Achievements

  • Successful playwright and actor.
  • Influential documentary presenter.
  • Autobiographical and fiction author.
  • Mental health advocate.
  • Recipient of numerous artistic and literary awards.

Life Timeline

1957 - Birth

Born on 24 August in Hampstead, London, England.

1979 - Career Beginnings

Joined the Cambridge Footlights where he met Hugh Laurie and Emma Thompson.

1981 - First TV Appearance

Made his television debut in "The Cellar Tapes" with Hugh Laurie.

1990 - Jeeves and Wooster

Starred in "Jeeves and Wooster" with Hugh Laurie, which became a major success.

1991 - First Novel

Published his first novel "The Liar" which was critically acclaimed.

2003 - Bipolar Diagnosis

Revealed his bipolar disorder and became a mental health advocate.

2006 - Hosting QI

Began hosting the quiz show "QI" which became one of Britain's most popular TV shows.

2010 - Autobiography

Published the first volume of his autobiography "The Fry Chronicles" which became a bestseller.

2018 - Marriage

Married his partner Elliott Spencer in a private ceremony.

Present - Continued Activity

Continues to work in acting, writing, and presenting with focus on mental health.
